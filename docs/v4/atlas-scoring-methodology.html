0 · How a stock's score is built

Each stock is scored by six lenses, each producing a 0–100. Four lenses are blended by weight into the composite; two (Valuation, Policy) are computed for display but carry 0% weight today. A lens that has no data is dropped and the remaining weights renormalise — scores are never imputed (Rule #0).

3 · Catalyst lens weight 20%

Composite = Earnings-strategy 0.55 + Capital-action 0.30 + Governance 0.15. Each filing scored × recency multiplier (≤90d 1.0 · ≤180d 0.8 · ≤365d 0.5 · >365d 0.3), summed per bucket, clamped 0–100. Source: foundation_staging.lens_filings (NSE announcements, 365-day lookback). atlas/lenses/compute/catalyst.py.

4 · Fundamental lens weight 25%

Composite = average of the present sub-scores × 5 (each 0–20; renormalised over present ones). Inputs derived as-of from foundation_staging.financials_quarterly (TTM = trailing 4 quarters) + financials_annual — both from XBRL (60-day lag on quarterly, 90-day on annual). fundamental.py + fundamental_pit.py.

Sub-scoreMaxExact rule (defaults)Input → exact source / formula
Profitability20 ROE: ≥20 20; ≥15 16; ≥12 12; ≥8 8; else 4. +2 if ROIC>15. roe = PAT_ttm ÷ equity ×100 (PAT from financials_quarterly.pat, equity from financials_annual.equity). roic = None (not sourced) → the +2 never fires.

Growth20 Rev YoY: >25 12; >15 9; >8 6; >0 3. EPS YoY: >30 +8; >15 +6; >5 +4; >0 +2. (ttm ÷ prior_ttm −1) on financials_quarterly.revenue and .eps.
Balance sheet20 D/E: net-cash/<0.3 10; <0.5 8; <1.0 6; <1.5 4; else 2. Current ratio & quick ratio each add up to +5. D/E from financials_quarterly.debt_equity_ratio (or borrowings÷equity). current_ratio, quick_ratio = None (not sourced).
Operating leverage20 High growth(>15)+expanding margin(>15)+low D/E → 20; high growth + one → 15; moderate(>8) → 10; positive → 5; declining → 0. composite of growth, op-margin, D/E above (all real).

Honest gaps (what is NOT real today, so it must be fixed or not shown):
  • Empty fundamentals: roic, roa, gross_margin, current_ratio, quick_ratio — available in XBRL, not extracted yet. (ROE has dirty tails from tiny-equity denominators → needs a validity guard.)
  • Valuation P/B & EV/EBITDA: no source wired — these dimensions are dropped (and Valuation is 0% weight anyway).
  • Institutional flow: mutual-fund holdings exist (6 months) but aren't wired, so most stocks sit at the neutral 50 inside the smart-money sub-score (~9% of the composite).
  • Policy: reframed out of scoring → informational alert layer + future news agent.
What is fully real today: the entire Technical lens, Catalyst, Flow-promoter & Flow-accumulation, and the bulk of Fundamental (profitability, margins, growth, D/E) — i.e. the large majority of the conviction score traces to real Kite/XBRL/NSE data.
