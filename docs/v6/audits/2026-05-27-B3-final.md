## te_60d — methodology note

All 34 ETFs use NIFTY500 as the benchmark (sectoral ETFs fall back to NIFTY500 since
sector-specific indices like NIFTYBANK are not in `atlas_benchmark_returns_cache`).
This inflates TE for sectoral ETFs vs their actual underlying index. Typical values:
- NIFTYBEES (Broad): te_60d ~0.02-0.05 (low, tracking Nifty50 ≈ NIFTY500)
- ITBEES (Sectoral): te_60d ~0.30 (high — IT sector returns diverge from NIFTY500)

This is directionally correct for ranking purposes (more volatile = higher TE).
